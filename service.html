Service

Through my work at the Aggieland Humane Society, I fostered newborn baby kittens at my house for 8 weeks. Providing care included feeding, cleaning, and playing with the kittens so that they would become more social with humans, as friendlier kittens are quicker to be adopted and find new homes.
